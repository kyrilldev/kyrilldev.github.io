The Assignment

The goal of this project was to produce a decent working race game to the end customer: other students. During this project I had the pleasure to work with 3 other game artists and 1 other developer. Above this section you can see a couple of my code highlights I encourage you to take a look at those. We came with a plan to make a total of 3 racings tracks, make the car drive like your driving in mario kart and to make a working checkpoint system so that the player could respawning when they crashed. I think the end result turned out pretty decent.
