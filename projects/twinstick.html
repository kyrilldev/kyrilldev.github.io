The Assignment

For this project we had 6 sprint AKA 6 weeks of development time. We started the project with a couple of bulletpoints given to us by the teachers that we had to follow. Like for a example a map-layout, map theme and game theme. In this project I collaborated with 3 artists and one other developer. We managed to deliver what I think is a pretty decent game having used Unity for only 6 months till that point. The goal of the game is to collect all the keycards spread throughout the level and elimate all the enemies scattered through the map without dying.
